Wave City

Wave City is one of India's largest Hi-Tech Cities, spread across an impressive 4500 acres. It is built on the Hi-Tech City concept and boasts of smart features like: Command Center, Street Lights Automation, Energy Metering, Water Metering, Intelligent Traffic Management etc.
